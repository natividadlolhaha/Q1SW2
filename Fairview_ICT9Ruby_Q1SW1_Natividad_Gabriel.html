Car Videography and Photography

I found cars exciting the moment I could walk. As a kid I used to ask my mom to take pictures of me when I saw a cool car because I found it awesome. Now that I am a teenager, I still enjoy the sight of cars and I am now able to photograph and record them with devices like my phone and my camera. I enjoy taking photos and recording cars because it brings out the beauty in these cars.

Why I enjoy videographing and photographing cars:

  • It can be done with friends which makes it more exciting
  • It makes me express my creativity by making me think of new angles and perspectives for the shot
  • I find it fun
